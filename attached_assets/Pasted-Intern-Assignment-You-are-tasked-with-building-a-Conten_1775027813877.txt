Evaluation Criteria
This assignment is deliberately open-ended. You will be evaluated on how you
solve the problem, not just whether it works.
Primary — Architecture and Performance
This is the most important dimension of the evaluation.
How did you model the analytics data, and why?
How fast are your analytics queries, and what did you do to keep them fast?
How do you handle high-frequency events?
Does your architecture hold up under scale, even if the current data volume is
small?
